// about me

From Sysadmin
to Security

Over 10 years in IT infrastructure — from desktop support at Lincoln Financial Group to systems administration at EHS Technologies supporting DoD compliance — gave me a deep foundation in how networks, hardware, and software fit together. Now I'm applying that operational knowledge directly to security.

At EHS Technologies, I support Risk Management Framework (RMF) processes, conduct vulnerability assessments with ACAS/Nessus, and produce Security Assessment Reports (SARs) for DoD enterprise systems. I hold a US Government Secret Clearance and am completing my B.S. in Information Sciences and Technology at Penn State (Minor: Security & Risk Analysis).

I hold CompTIA Security+, Network+, and the Secure Infrastructure Specialist (CSIS) designation, and I'm actively working toward additional certifications in cloud and offensive security.
